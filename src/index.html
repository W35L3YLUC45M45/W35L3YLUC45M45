Honors in Multimedia Production I

Awarded with honors for getting one of the highest grades of the entire course having mastered mainly the use of video editing tools such as Adobe Premiere and Adobe After Effects for a large-scale project where we had to carry out the production process of a short film. Awarded with honors for getting one of the highest grades of the whole course by understanding and putting into practice the fundamentals of PHP, a backend programming language. Honors in Physics Simulation

Awarded with honors for getting one of the highest grades of the whole course by learning and putting into practice many different aspects of physics simulation. These aspects range from developing 2D videogames to working with genetics algorithms. In the case of 2D videogame development, we used the p5.js library (or Processing) along with the Box2D physics library to develop games like Angry Birds and a billiards game where we had to program not only the rules' logics, but also the collisions for both games. Lastly, regarding the genetics algorithms, we learned and put into practice all of the concepts of this field through projects done on Processing and even Python, where we had to document, justify and experiment with many different algorithms and the results that presented the generations of the simulations we ran through a script.
